## About My Project! 

"The Love Yourself Lab" is an uplifting webpage where I designed for it to generate daily self-love affirmation. Built with **HTML**, **CSS**, and **JavaScript**.

This project serves as a gentle reminder that you are worthy, capable, and deserving of love, ESPECIALLY from yourself. 

## Why Self-Love Matters...

In a world that constantly demands more from us, self-love is not a luxury-it's a necessity. When practicing self-love, you:

-**Build confidence** within yourself & your abilities. 
-**Reduce stress and the feeling of being anxious** by being kind to yourself instead of making feelings worse. 
-**Attract postitive relationships** when you respect yourself first!
-**Practice resilience and compassion** when handling and navigating challenges.
